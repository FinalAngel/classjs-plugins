Options

» index: 2
» expanded: true
» event: 'hover'
» easing: 'swing'
» duration: 0

» grouping: false
» forceClose: true
» disableAnchors: false
» autoHeight: false
» cls: {}
» lang: {}

Methods

» my.accordion.toggle(index);
» my.accordion.show(, [index]);
» my.accordion.hide(, [index]);

Events and Callbacks

» $(document).on('accordion-toggle', function...);
» my.accordion.callbacks.toggle = function...

available events and callbacks are:
toggle, show, hide

check the console for debug information on events.
