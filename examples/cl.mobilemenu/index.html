This is a generic navigation. Resizes the window to be below 540 pixels and the mobile menu is shown.

Options

» index: 2
» easing: 'swing'
» duration: 1000
» bound: 539

» ratio: 80 / 100
» offset: {}
» cls: {}
» overlay: '«div class...'

Methods

» my.mobilemenu.toggle();
» my.mobilemenu.show();
» my.mobilemenu.hide();

Events and Callbacks

» $(document).on('mobilemenu-toggle', function...);
» my.mobilemenu.callbacks.toggle = function...

available events and callbacks are:
toggle, show, hide

check the console for debug information on events.
